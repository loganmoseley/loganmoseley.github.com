Faye's Shift

    • Play as Faye in a 2.5D platformer in which you phase between the lava and ice phases in order to navigate past obstacles and progress through the world. The two phases have distinct personalities.

    Developed by Robert Baldwin, Andrew Dolce, Michael "Z" Goddard, Greg Lane, Logan Moseley, and Kyle Okaly. RPI, Spring 2009.

    Bedlam Horror

    Psychological thriller. You are Paul, mental patient. Get out!

    Download the game: Bedlam Horror.zip.
    Setup and running: readme.txt
    \Release\Bedlam Horror.exe

    Developed by Edward Corlew, Logan Moseley, and others. RPI, Spring 2008.

    Apollo 13

    A top-down space shooter. Your mission: shoot the bad guys.

    Download the game: Apollo 13.zip.
    Run the game: \Apollo13\src\run_game.bat
    Requires Pygame: http://pygame.org/download.shtml.

    Developed by Jonathon Azzara, David Love, Logan Moseley, and others. RPI, Fall 2007.

    Race to the Finish

    A single-player racer, complete with a gear-car. Your goal: complete a lap in the least amount of time.

    (You must stay on the track to get credit for completing a lap.)

    Download the game: Race to the Finish.zip.
    Run the game: \Apollo13\src\run_game.bat
    Requires Panda3d: http://panda3d.org/download.php.

    Developed by Jonathon Azzara, David Love, Logan Moseley, and others. RPI, Fall 2007.
